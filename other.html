DIY Opportunities

Items you can do yourselves — no contractor needed:
Item DIY Savings Notes
Receptacles + switches (#19) $100–$150 materials only Swap ~25 standard outlets and switches for Decora style. No rewiring — just plate replacement.
Repaint living room + stairway (#20) Save $1,500–$3,500 vs. contractor Standard interior paint. Budget for materials only if DIY.
Repaint lower mudroom (#29) Save $400–$1,100 vs. contractor Small space, straightforward repaint.
Rehang art (#28) Save $500–$2,000 vs. professional Use a laser level. Gallery walls take time but no skill.
Deck restaining (exterior) Save $800–$1,500 on labor Top surface and beam ends are accessible. Underside spray is also DIY with a good HVLP or airless sprayer.
Entry door restain (exterior GOOD) Save $200–$400 on labor Strip and restain existing doors. One weekend.
IKEA PAX closet (bedroom) Save $400–$600 on installation Designed for DIY assembly. Plan the layout carefully first.
Slider cladding paint (exterior bridge) Save $200–$400 on labor Aluminum bonding primer + spray or brush paint. Test one first.
Window frame painting (exterior) Save $300–$600 on labor If keeping existing windows — clean, prime, paint frames. Patience required.
Total potential DIY savings: $4,400–$9,750 — not trivial. These are all items that genuinely do not require a contractor if you are willing to spend the time.
