Primary Bathroom

GOOD · BETTER · BEST · $0 to $4,400

Fully deferrable. None of these options block or are blocked by anything else. BETTER tier (tile only) is a standalone 1–2 day contractor job that can happen any time. BEST tier is most economical if done during the bedroom renovation, but it is not required then — it can be deferred to any point.
The issue: The existing tile is garish — visually dominant in the wrong direction for the aesthetic we're building. The countertop is dated but functional. Neither is a structural problem. This is purely a cosmetic decision about how much disruption and cost is worth the improvement.

Three Tiers

GOOD

Do Nothing

$0 No cost, no disruption
  • Accept existing tile as-is
  • Keep existing countertop
  • Bathroom is functional
  • No contractor needed
  • Zero schedule impact

Best if: Budget is tight or the bathroom is low priority. The tile is an aesthetic problem, not a functional one. Guests will use it; it won't break.

BETTER

Replace the Tile

$300–$800 Materials + labor. 1–2 days.
  • Remove existing garish tile
  • Install new neutral backsplash tile
  • Keep existing countertop
  • Significant visual improvement
  • Low disruption — tile contractor, 1 day
  • Can be done independently, any time

Best if: The tile is the primary visual problem and the countertop is tolerable. Highest impact per dollar spent in the bathroom.

BEST

Tile + Countertop

$1,800–$4,400 Full refresh. Cabinet options below.
  • Replace garish tile
  • New countertop (quartz or granite, 6')
  • Reface vanity — new doors and drawer fronts, keep existing box
  • Complete visual transformation
  • Coordinate with bedroom construction

Best if: The countertop and cabinet are also problems. Do during bedroom renovation — contractor already mobilized. If the cabinet box is water-damaged, full replacement is an alternative at the high end of the range.

Budget Detail

Tier Scope Low High

BEST tier cabinet note: Refacing keeps the existing box and replaces only the doors and drawer fronts — less demo, less cost, same visual result as a new cabinet. If inspection reveals the box is water-damaged or structurally unsound, full replacement is the fallback. Inspect before ordering materials.

Timing

Do BEST tier during the bedroom renovation. Your contractor will already be mobilized. Adding bathroom cabinet and tile work to that visit costs significantly less than a separate trip. BETTER tier (tile only) can be done independently at any time — it's a standalone 1-day job that doesn't need to be coordinated with anything else.

What This Does Not Include

  • New faucet or shower hardware (separate line item if desired)
  • Shower tile replacement (larger scope, not in current plan)
  • Toilet replacement
  • Plumbing relocation — note: plumbing cluster already completed, no plumbing work required for any of these options
