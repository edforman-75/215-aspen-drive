Great Room — Furniture Plan

832 sq ft · Open-Plan Living / Kitchen / Dining · Room & Board

Fully deferrable. No construction work gates this project. All furniture can be ordered and delivered at any time — next month or two years from now. Start whenever the budget is ready. The only reason to order sooner is the 6–8 week lead time on Room & Board pieces: if you want furniture by a specific date, count back from there. Slight incentive to consolidate the great room order with bedroom furniture once construction ends — same delivery trip reduces shipping charges.

Design Brief

What stays: 1918 Steinway Model O piano, two earthy green/brown carpets (9'×10' and 8'×12'), wall textiles, triangle table, buffet, wrought iron chandelier (dining area), wrought iron counter stools (kitchen island)

What goes: All existing furniture (pine/oak pieces)

Palette: Walnut wood, oatmeal/warm gray upholstery, cognac/camel leather accents, matte black metal, White Dove walls (BM OC-17)

Alternative Option: Use Paul McCobb Planner Group maple case goods (coffee table, end tables, console) instead of Room & Board walnut pieces. Combines with same upholstered furniture below. Can create unified design vocabulary with bedroom. → View Planner Group Great Room Option
