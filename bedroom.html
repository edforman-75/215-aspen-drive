Primary Bedroom — Furniture Plan

L-Shaped Room with Alcove · Five Options: Hudson / Thuma / Vintage / Planner Group / Vintage+Steel

Project Overview

L-shaped room: main section 172" × 190" (14'4" × 15'10") with alcove 70" × 70" (5'10" × 5'10"). Entry wall 170" wide. Bed wall 240" total (170" main + 70" alcove). Construction raises ceiling from ~7' to ~9'7" (matching great room), removes beam/soffit, enlarges alcove window, replaces carpet with hardwood. All existing pine furniture goes. Wall textiles, Persian/Heriz rugs, Chinese carved chest stay.

Design Direction: Continue walnut + performance fabric vocabulary from great room. Hudson collection (built in West Virginia by Caperton) is the primary plan below. Quinn chair appears in both rooms. Handmade textiles and antique rugs provide warmth and history.

Testing Swatches

Order ALL swatches and test them together in the actual bedroom:

  1. Lay Persian rugs on engineered hardwood samples
  2. Place wall paint sample (White Dove) against wall near rugs
  3. Set walnut wood swatches on rugs to see color interaction
  4. Drape fabric swatches over walnut samples with rugs underneath
  5. Test in morning, midday, and evening light
  6. Hold swatches near existing wall textiles

CRITICAL: Walnut can vary significantly between batches. Order swatches from EACH Hudson piece to confirm consistency before purchasing.
