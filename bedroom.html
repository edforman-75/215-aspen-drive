L-Shaped Room with Alcove · Hudson Collection · Room & Board

Project Overview

L-shaped room: main rectangle ~195" × 170" (16'3" × 14'2") with alcove 98" × 70" (8'2" × 5'10"). Construction raises ceiling from ~7' to ~9'7" (matching great room), removes beam/soffit, enlarges alcove window, replaces carpet with hardwood. All existing pine furniture goes. Wall textiles, Persian/Heriz rugs, Chinese carved chest stay.

Design Direction: Continue walnut + performance fabric vocabulary from great room. Hudson collection (built in West Virginia by Caperton) ties to Hudson media cabinet in living room. Quinn chair appears in both rooms. Handmade textiles and antique rugs provide warmth and history.

4 Zones

Zone A — Sleep (Long Wall, 172")

King bed centered on 172" wall. ~46" clearance each side for nightstands. Bed faces sliding door — wake up to natural light and trees.

Zone B — Storage (Wall-Hanging Wall, 98")

56"W dresser centered on 98" wall, below large Mayan/Central American textile. 21" clearance each side.

Zone C — Reading Nook (Alcove, 98" × 70")

Quinn chair angled toward enlarged window. Small side table + ceramic lamp. Built-in shelves above for books. Persian rug underfoot.

Zone D — Dumping / Transition

Chinese carved chest (40"W × 20"D × 22"H) at foot of bed. Woven laundry hamper near bathroom opening. Wall hooks near hallway entry.
