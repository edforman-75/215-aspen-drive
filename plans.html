Key Immovable Elements

Freestanding Fireplace

80" wide × 41" stone depth + 16" hearth shelf. Opening faces deck wall. Back of stone 7'6" from passage wall. Nearest side 6' from TV wall. Passage on all four sides — the visual heart of the room.

Kitchen Island

150" long curved concrete counter with cooktop, framed by two timber posts. Asymmetric shape — 31" straight section at passage end, then curves to 34" apex depth. Inner edge 83" from cabinet wall with 54" passageway.

Stair Opening

42" wide × 12.5' long, running along passage wall from TV wall corner. Open rail — no solid wall below chest height.

Piano

1918 Steinway Model O (~5'11"). East corner where deck wall meets TV wall, facing Baldy mountain views through windows on two sides.
